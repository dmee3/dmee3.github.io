iMusic

November 2014

iMusic is an iOS music player with added Facebook and YouTube integration. Aside from playing music stored on the device, users can view a list of Facebook friends who use the app, see what their friends have been listening to, and browse/search YouTube to find music videos.

The application was developed for iOS by a 3-man team for Ohio State's Mobile Application Development course. I and my teammates distributed the workload at regular intervals based roughly on an agile development plan. My most important contribution to the project was the implementation of a custom class to keep track of audio playback, song queues, and other information related to the music player itself.
